## SPEC TO ADD

**The three lead magnets = the SAME three named in the free community About page's
✅ "what you unlock for free" list.** Tie each to ONE of the ICP's top three
bottlenecks so each solves a different real problem. Each lead magnet contains, in
order:

1. **Course name** — max 50 chars (Skool's limit). Suby/Hormozi naming: proof
   number, fear-of-loss, or mechanism + speed/ease; curiosity + specificity.
2. **Course description** — max 500 chars, direct-response, FOMO, benefit-forward.
3. **THREE content pages** — each a FULL lesson, 700 to 1200 words. Each page:
   a one-line **bold takeaway**; thorough teaching (what, why, exact how in steps);
   **two or more worked EXAMPLES** as tables or boxed calcs with real numbers; a
   step-by-step **walkthrough**; a **common-mistakes** beat; a **recap/your-move**.
   Scannable throughout (sub-headings, bold, bullets, numbered steps, tables, `> `
   callouts). Depth AND scannability. A thin ten-line page is a FAIL.
4. **TWO worksheets** — structured fill-in exercises, not bare blanks: a `*Purpose:*`
   line, 2 to 4 titled PARTS, inline guidance + a worked example, labeled fill-in
   fields and small fill-in TABLES, `☐` self-check, and a bold `**Done when:**` line.
   Exports clean to a one-page PDF.
5. **A Loom script** — 400 to 650 words, spoken in the client's voice: hook the
   bottleneck, what they'll get, walk the lessons, nudge the worksheets, soft CTA to
   the roadmap call or DM keyword.

**Golden rules:** NO em dashes (use "to" for ranges); no fluff/filler; compliance-safe
where the niche needs it (research-use-only, no medical or income claims, a
"results not typical / education not advice" line where money or health is implied);
the client's real voice; grounded in research + Offers/ICP; real proof or [PROOF:].
Placeholders `[BOOKING LINK]` and `[COMMUNITY NAME]` stay literal.
