# Hormozi $100M Leads Playbook (mined cover to cover, all 309 pages)

The advertising half of the Hormozi canon. hormozi-offers.md governs WHAT we
sell; this governs how strangers find out about it. Governs lead magnets,
ad copy, content plans, outreach scripts, email volume math, referral
mechanics, and every growth recommendation we put in client deliverables.

## Engaged leads, not leads

A lead is only a lead when it ENGAGES: gives contact info, books a call,
replies, shows up. Everything we build optimizes for engaged leads. Two
things make strangers engage: an offer (they pay) or a lead magnet (free).

## Lead magnets (the 7-step build)

A lead magnet is a complete solution to a NARROW problem that reveals the
larger problem the core offer solves. Build:
1. Pick a narrow, meaningful problem (solving it MUST surface the next one)
2. Pick the type: reveal a problem (audit/assessment), free trial/sample,
   or one step of a multi-step process
3. Pick delivery: software, information, service, physical
4. Test names/titles like headlines (the packaging IS the marketing)
5. Make it easy to consume (shorter, faster, on their platform)
6. Make it so good they'd pay: "give away the secrets, sell the
   implementation"
7. Make it easy for them to tell you they want the next thing: CTA with
   scarcity, urgency, and a REASON WHY ("because" doubles compliance even
   with weak reasons)
Higher-cost lead magnets (free LLC setup, free consult) are legitimate:
judge by CAC math, not sticker cost.

## The Core Four (the only four ways one person can advertise)

Warm vs cold, public vs private:
1. **Warm outreach** (private, people who know you): 10 steps. Build the
   list from every contact source, personalize, A-C-A every reply
   (Acknowledge their words, Compliment a trait, Ask the next question),
   make the offer with the value equation (dream outcome, likelihood, time,
   effort), "first five free" in exchange for testimonial + referrals,
   the 9-word re-engagement email ("Are you still looking to [dream
   outcome]?").
2. **Post free content** (public, warm then cold): the content unit =
   HOOK (topic + headline + format), RETAIN (lists, steps, stories),
   REWARD (deliver on the hook's promise, fully). Topics from own life,
   others' questions, and what worked before. Give until they ask; then
   integrated (in-content) or intermittent (periodic promo) asks; ONE CTA.
   78% of best customers consumed 2+ long-form pieces before buying:
   engineer that journey deliberately.
3. **Cold outreach** (private, strangers): buy/scrape/build lists,
   personalize the first line, lead with BIG FAST VALUE, write at a
   3rd-grade reading level (+50% replies), automate for volume, follow up
   multiple times, expect low response and out-volume it.
4. **Paid ads** (public, strangers): see the ad section below.
Order for a new business: warm outreach -> content (more time than money)
or cold outreach/paid ads (more money than time). Pick ONE and max it out;
methods compound.

## Paid ads: the copy anatomy

Every ad = **Callout + Value (the three Ws) + CTA**.
- **Callouts** grab the right people: verbal (label the avatar, their
  problem, their location, "if you..." qualifiers) and nonverbal (contrast,
  likeness: people like them, familiar scenes).
- **What-Who-When value framework**: run the offer through all 8 value
  elements (dream outcome vs nightmare, likelihood of success vs risk of
  failure, speed vs time cost, ease vs effort/sacrifice), from the WHO
  perspectives (self, spouse, kids, rivals, peers) and WHEN timelines
  (past pain, present, future). "As specific as you can, but no
  specific-er." 30 callouts x 10 ads = an infinite ad grid.
- **CTA**: quick and easy, tell them exactly what to do next.
- Landing page must be CONGRUENT with the ad (same promise, same look).
- Top 0.1% of advertisers test 11x more creative than everyone else. You
  can't scale a mediocre ad; make more ads to find the exceptional ones.
- Free content that performed well converts to winning paid ads; UGC and
  testimonials are the cheapest steady ad source.

## The money math (governs every growth recommendation)

- **LTGP:CAC must exceed 3:1.** LTGP = lifetime GROSS profit (revenue minus
  direct delivery cost), not revenue. Below 3:1 diagnose with ONE question:
  "Do my engaged leads have the problem I solve and the money to spend?"
  No = advertising problem. Yes but not buying = SALES problem. Yes and
  buying but too few = advertising problem. Never fire the sales team for
  an ads problem or vice versa.
- **Client Financed Acquisition**: engineer 30-day cash collected > CAC +
  COGS, usually via an immediate upsell, so every customer finances the
  next. This is how Hormozi scaled every company past $1M/mo in year one
  with no outside funding.
- Test budget: be willing to lose 2x your normal 30-day customer cash
  collected while learning. "You won't be earning, you'll be learning."
- Three phases: track money (learn) -> lose money (scale past comfort,
  fine if LTGP:CAC holds) -> print money.

## More Better New (when growth stalls)

1. **More**: Rule of 100: 100 primary actions per day for 100 days (100
   reach-outs, or 100 min of content, or 100 min/$100 of ads). Advanced:
   Open To Goal: work until the outcome number is hit, no matter what.
2. **Better**: find the CONSTRAINT (the step where most leads drop off) and
   test there; one test per week per platform; log every result; four
   failed attempts to beat the control = move to the next constraint.
   Front of funnel usually beats back (callout, headline, offer, CTA).
   Under $1M/yr profit: more first. Over $1M: better first.
3. **New**: Size of the Pie Fallacy: your niche is not saturated, your
   slice of one platform is. Order: new placements on your platform ->
   new platforms -> new core four activity.

## Lead getters (scale past one person)

Do the core four once to GET lead getters; then they do it for you forever.
1. **Customer referrals**: goodwill (value minus price) drives word of
   mouth. Referral growth equation: % referred monthly minus % churned
   monthly = compounding rate. Six ways to give more value (sell better-fit
   customers only, underpromise/overdeliver, force the actions your best
   customers took, make wins faster: 48-hour first impression + BAMFAM:
   book-a-meeting-from-a-meeting + pad timelines to always deliver early,
   keep improving from complaint data, always tell them what to buy next).
   Seven ways to ASK: pay CAC to referrer or friend, split it two-sided
   (Dropbox/PayPal), ask at checkout ("who else could you do this with?
   people who do it with someone get 3x results": half that rep's sales),
   referral as negotiation chip, referral events (bring-a-friend, spouse
   challenge), talk about it in all content always, unlockable bonuses for
   referral + testimonial. Ask WHO they know, not IF. 25-30% extra signups
   just from asking at purchase.
2. **Employees**: internal core four to recruit. Train with the 3Ds:
   Document (checklist), Demonstrate (you do it in front of them),
   Duplicate (they do it in front of you; fix the checklist, not the
   person). If they follow directions and get the wrong result, the
   directions are wrong. Payroll / engaged leads = cost per lead; same
   3:1 math.
3. **Agencies**: use them to LEARN, not to depend on. Opening script: "I
   want to do what you do in my business... work with you 6 months, pay
   extra for you to explain your decisions, then train my team and drop
   to consulting." One good-enough agency to learn a platform, one elite
   agency to master it; cancel when your team beats theirs.
4. **Affiliates**: independent businesses that sell to your ICP. Six steps:
   find them ("who's got my leads?": what do my best customers buy, where
   do they go, what do they do), make them a money-making offer (callout +
   value elements + CTA, same as any ad), qualify them by making them
   invest (buy the product and/or pay 10-20% of first-year affiliate
   earnings for certification: if they pay, they pay attention), pay from
   max allowable CAC with a three-tier structure (25% for agreeing, 50%
   for activating, 100% for sustaining: blended payout drops, ratio
   improves), launch with whisper-tease-shout (whisper = curiosity
   callouts weeks out; tease = reveal + value elements + date; shout =
   CTA + bonuses + scarcity + urgency, escalating to launch), keep them
   advertising by INTEGRATION: they give away your lead magnet with their
   core offer, sell your lead magnet, or sell your core offer for a rev
   share. Pay forever, never cap payouts. Whisper-tease-shout is how you
   launch ANYTHING, not just affiliates.

## Doctrine lines worth quoting

- "Advertising always works, it's only a matter of efficiency."
- "You cannot lose if you do not quit." (the many-sided die: every roll
  turns a red side green; volume negates luck)

  method broken. Diagnose effort before diagnosing strategy.

  -> repeat with employees as the target.
