# VSL Funnel Swipe File (proven cold-traffic funnels, studied 2026-07)

Real funnels from the top of the info industry, broken down for pattern-modeling.
Steal SHAPES, never sentences. When writing a funnel, pick the 2-3 entries closest
to the client's market and model the structure, then out-specific them with the
client's proof.

## 1. The Empowered Man (marriage coaching, cold Meta traffic, call funnel) — GOLD STANDARD

The owner's benchmark for "this would convert cold traffic". Full anatomy:

- **Eyebrow**: quoted mini-promise: "Marriage On The Brink Of Divorce? Fix It In 30 Days Or Less"
- **Headline**: time-boxed micro-commitment + outcome + deadline: "Give Me 5 Minutes
  And I'll Show You Exactly How To Save Your Marriage In The Next 30 Days" (the
  "Give me [tiny time] and I'll [big outcome] in [timeframe]" shape)
- **Subheadline** packs FIVE jobs in 3 sentences: named vehicle ("the underground
  system"), count-proof ("5,375 Aussie dads"), dream outcomes stacked, objection
  kills ("without begging, therapy, or talking about it"), open-loop scarcity
  ("first time ever open to the public, only for a limited time")
- **VSL** with captions burned in, then **outcome-worded CTA button** ("SAVE YOUR
  MARRIAGE NOW", never "Learn More"), repeated identically 6+ times down the page
- **Laurel proof strip** directly under CTA: 4 stats (5,375+ marriages saved,
  250,000+ views, 100,000+ coaching sessions, 1,000+ 5-star reviews)
- **Quote-style section header** ("THIS MAY BE THE SINGLE MOST POWERFUL SYSTEM...")
- **Pain block**: second-person scenes, one line each ("You're successful at work,
  but your home life is falling apart... A house that feels more like a war zone
  than a sanctuary"), then "Sound familiar? You're not alone."
- **Not-your-fault bridge** + market stat, then enemy positioning ("traditional
  marriage counselling doesn't work for men like us")
- **Founder story** with real photos, vulnerable one-liner in handwriting font,
  "learn from my mistakes"
- **Named system reveal**: 'Marriage Rescue System', then timeboxed outcome bullets
  ("In just 8 weeks we'll help you to: ...")
- **Named free session**: 'Control Your Destiny' coaching session, "valued at $997,
  at no cost to you" (name the call + anchor its value)
- **Trust line under every CTA**: "Trusted By 5,375+ High Performance Men"
- **Video testimonial wall**: 6 videos, each captioned with the client's verbatim
  pain quote ("She didn't love me anymore", "Not the fcking business again!")
- **5-step system breakdown** as numbered cards (Clarity, Plan, Action,
  Accountability, Change), each step = what happens + what you get
- **FB-review screenshot wall** headed "REAL MEN. FAMILIES. RESULTS."
- **8-reasons cards** (photo + one outcome sentence each)
- **Movement close**: "It's More Than A Program... It's A Movement."

## 2. Viral Coach / Daniel Iles (1M views guaranteed, ~$3M/mo on cold VSL traffic)

- Eyebrow IS the guarantee: "1 Million Views Guaranteed."
- Qualification CTA: "SEE IF YOU QUALIFY" (prospect applies to THEM)
- 4-metric proof row (15.5M avg client views, 1M+ leads, 3,000+ owners, 250+ niches)
- Case studies as number stories (Dr. Blake: 500k followers, 79k leads/month)
- 4-step process section; guarantee restated as banner near close

## 3. Cole Gordon / closers.io (recruiting offer, hard gate)

- Eyebrow is a GATE: "If You Do Not Specifically Own A Coaching/Course Business Or
  Marketing Agency - Leave This Page Now" (repels wrong traffic, flatters right)
- Headline = give-us-time + do-it-for-you + guarantee: "Give Me 7 Days... And I'll
  Place You With A Proven Appointment Setter And If They Don't Book You 40 Sales
  Calls In The First Month - You Don't Pay!"
- The guarantee IS the funnel; everything else supports it
- Main site: eyebrow "Unlock the Power of Top 1% Sales Talent", named-client photo
  wall (Graziosi, Kern, Abraham), 4-step process, "work on their business, not in it"

## 4. King Kong / Sabri Suby (agency, personality-heavy)

- Headline dares: "Like Steroids* For Business (*But it's 100% legal)"
- CTA buttons in-voice: "Hit the damn button"
- Proof: "$7.8 Billion in reported client revenue", "4.8 stars out of 7,608 reviews"
- Guarantee: "WE BEAT YOUR RESULTS BY 30% OR WE WORK FOR FREE" (with honest conditions)

  when the market is corporate.

## 5. Acquisition.com / Hormozi

## Cross-cutting rules (what ALL the winners do)

1. The eyebrow works: a gate, a guarantee, or a quoted promise. Never decoration.
2. Headline shapes that repeat across winners: "Give me [tiny time] and I'll
   [outcome] in [timeframe]" | guarantee-as-headline | yes-question | category claim.
3. Subheadline carries the mechanism NAME + count-proof + "without [objections]".
4. CTA button says the outcome ("SAVE YOUR MARRIAGE NOW"), repeats identically,
   and has a trust line directly beneath it every time.
5. Proof strip within one scroll of the video: 3-4 big numbers.
6. Name everything: the system, the free call, the roadmap. Named things feel real
   and anchor value ("valued at $997").
7. Qualify hard somewhere: a gate line, an application, or minimum requirements.
   Repelling wrong traffic raises show rates and close rates.
8. Testimonials carry verbatim pain quotes as their headlines, not praise.
9. A numbered system breakdown (3-5 steps), each step ending in what they GET.
10. One honest disclaimer/anti-hype line builds more trust than ten claims.

## Cold-traffic VSL benchmarks (for the funnel's expectations section)

- VSL completion above 30% on cold traffic = healthy; below = weak lead
- 60%+ of viewers should survive the first 5 minutes; the "why watch" must land
  inside the first 30-60 seconds
- Application-to-call 30-60%; call-to-close 10-25% (high-ticket coaching norms)
