# Sell Like A Doctor: The Sales Call Script (Sell Like Crazy Phase 7, mined line by line)

Governs every sales script, call framework, and setter/closer doc we write.
A doctor doesn't pitch; a doctor examines, diagnoses, and prescribes. The
prospect should feel examined and understood, never "sold".

## Pre-call: the survey filter

Booking requires answering qualifying questions (business, revenue, problem,
why now). The survey (a) filters tyre-kickers, (b) arms the closer with the
prospect's own words, (c) starts the commitment ladder. Never write a booking
flow without qualification questions.

## The call structure, in order

1. **Frame + permission**: set the agenda, get agreement ("I'll ask some
   questions to see if/how we can help; if it makes sense we'll talk next
   steps; fair?").
2. **Find the Why**: dig beneath the surface goal to the real driver. Ask
   "why is that important to you?" until you hit the emotional floor (the
   kids, the mortgage, the pride). The Why is what they buy.
3. **The Dan Sullivan question**: "If we were talking three years from
   today, what has to have happened for you to feel happy with your
   progress?" Paints the dream outcome in THEIR words; you now sell the gap.
4. **The Admission**: get them to say out loud what hasn't worked and that
   they can't close the gap alone on their current path. They must diagnose
   themselves; a self-spoken problem cannot be argued with.
5. **Deliver value LIVE**: give a genuine insight or quick win on the call
   (doctor demonstrating competence during the examination).
6. **Commitment check**: "on a scale of 1-10, how committed are you to
   fixing this in the next 90 days?" Anything under 8: explore or disqualify.
7. **The 2-minute Prescription**: a SHORT, tailored overview of the program
   as the treatment for THEIR diagnosis, mapped to their Why and their gap.
   Two minutes maximum; prescriptions are concise, pitches ramble.
8. **The Close**:
   - Temperature checks: "does this feel like what you've been looking
     for?" / "Other than money, is there any reason you wouldn't move
     forward?" / "do you feel like you'd be a good candidate for this?"
   - State the price WITH PRIDE in one confident run-on sentence, anchored
     against the stacked value, no flinch, no pause before the number.
   - The set-up fee sits on ALL packages, then waive it as a Fast Action
     Bonus for deciding today (real, ethical urgency).
   - Then SILENCE. The person who speaks first, loses. Hold it.
   - Card collection is logistics, delivered as such: "great, I'll grab some
     details and we'll get you started."

## Objection principles

- Objections voiced before the price are information; after the price they
  are requests for reassurance. Re-anchor to their Why and their Admission:
  "you told me X was costing you Y and you can't fix it alone; what's
  changed in the last two minutes?"
- Never discount. Add a bonus tied to the specific objection instead
  (hormozi-offers.md bonus rules apply on live calls too).

## Where this maps in our pipeline

- **Sales scripts deliverable**: this IS the master structure; adapt the
  questions to the client's offer and ICP verbatims.
- **VSL structure**: the VSL is this call in one-to-many form: agitate (find
  the why), self-diagnosis moments ("sound familiar?"), value live (teach the
  mechanism), prescription (the offer), temperature-check FAQ, close.
- **Onboarding/booking pages**: qualification survey questions come from the
  survey-filter rules.
