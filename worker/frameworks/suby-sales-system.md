# Suby's 17-Step Secret Selling System + Selling The Free Call

The spine of EVERY sales message we write: landing pages, VSLs, sales letters,
webinar pitches, long emails. ($400M in client sales run through this order.)

## The 17 steps, in order

1. **Call out your audience** (top of ad/page/letter: the eyebrow callout)
2. **Demand attention** with a big-promise headline (Enquirer energy: "How To
   Lose 10Kgs In 6 Weeks Without Exercise Or Dieting... Guaranteed!")
3. **Back up the big promise** with a straightforward subheadline
4. **Create irresistible intrigue**: write 10-20 extra headlines, trim to the
   best SIX as fascination bullets, each hitting a different pain/fear/hope/
   dream + intrigue ("The Little-Known Secret to ___ that ___ Don't Want You
   to Know")
5. **Shine a floodlight on the problem**: read their mind ("yeah, that's
   exactly how I feel"), agitate hard (pain motivates more than pleasure),
   and recount in vivid detail what they've tried before and why it failed
6. **Provide the solution** and PROVE it's different from everything that
   failed them
7. **Show credentials**: results, case studies, prestigious names, customer
   counts, press, awards. Skepticism is at its peak right here.
8. **Detail the benefits**: features tell, benefits sell; talk ONLY in
   benefits (from the features->benefits detail sheet)
9. **Social proof**: third-party validation, research stats, authority quotes
10. **Make the Godfather offer** (clear, value-based, concise, persuasive,
    irresistible; see godfather-offer.md)
11. **Add bonuses**: desirable but non-essential sweeteners they simply want
12. **Stack the value**: total everything up with per-item worth, paint the
    picture
13. **Reveal the price** against the stacked value; for lead-gen, put a
    DOLLAR VALUE on the free consult
14. **Inject scarcity** (genuine only: expiry, doors closing, X left, price
    hike coming)
15. **Give a powerful guarantee** (longer guarantee = fewer returns; risk
    moves from buyer to seller)
16. **Call to action**: a COMMAND, one action only, zero ambiguity
17. **Close with a P.S.** carrying a warning (cost of inaction) + offer
    reminder + CTA + scarcity. The P.S. is the third most-read element.

## Selling the free call (the booking-page / offer-section pattern)

Never just "book a free call". The call is a PRODUCT:
- Name it + attach value: "Free No-Obligation 45-Minute 'Property Tycoon'
  Investment Strategy Consultation (Valued at $1,000)"
- "During your call you'll discover..." + 5-6 fascination bullets, each a
  number/insider tip/trap/how-to with intrigue
- Bold the first sentence of each bullet, expand in plain text after
- Detail the duration AND deliverables so the value is believable; the call
  must stand alone as valuable even if they never buy
- Sold this way, the conversation closes up to 80% of qualified prospects

## Traffic temperature (message-to-market match)

- The Larger Market Formula: 3% buying now, 17% gathering info, 60% not yet
  aware they have the problem. Write for more than the 3%.
- Cold = Tinder traffic (stranger: lead with value, HVCO, zero commitment
  asks). Warm = second date (they know you: named-session offers work).
  Hot = Netflix & chill (past buyers: direct offers).
- "The temperature of your marketing message must match the temperature of
  your traffic." Cold-traffic funnel pages must SELL the click-worthy value
  exchange, not propose marriage on the first date.
