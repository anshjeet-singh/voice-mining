# Skool PAID community About page — swipe (operator-approved format)

The paid Skool community About page is a **value-stack offer page**, NOT the free-value page.
Model these two operator-approved examples EXACTLY: emoji-led scannable lines, a hook headline
with a big proof number, an early-bird price, the promise + a mechanism they don't teach, a
✅ green-tick value stack where every line ends with a ($dollar anchor), 🎁 gift-box bonuses
each with a ($value), a Total-Value-vs-price line, a price-increase urgency line, a soft DM
CTA, then an ANNUAL PLAN block (Everything Above + extras + lifetime lock) and JOIN NOW.

Pull the real deliverables, price, and guarantee from the approved Offers doc + Course Outline;
value anchors are realistic; `[COMMUNITY NAME]` is a placeholder but prices are real.

## The skeleton to reproduce (both variations)

1. `🏆/🏦 [HOOK headline with a big proof or outcome number]`
2. `‼️ Early Bird: $[monthly]/mo`
3. `[1-2 lines: the promise + the mechanism "they don't teach"]`
4. `What you get:` then ✅ lines, EACH ending in `($value)` — real pillars from the Offers doc
5. `Bonus:` then 🎁 lines, EACH ending in `($value)`
6. `🏷️ Total Value: $[sum] — For Just $[monthly]/mo!`
7. `‼️ Price Increases to $[higher]/mo (spots filling fast)`
8. `💬 Not Sure? DM "[KEYWORD]" To Ask Q's`
9. `🔥 ANNUAL PLAN` → `✅ Everything Above` + 3-5 more `✅ ... ($value)` + `✅ Lifetime Price Locked In`
10. `🏷️ Total Value: $[sum] — For Just $[annual]/yr`
11. `💥 JOIN NOW`

The FREE community About page is different: green-tick FREE value (3 named lead magnets) +
🎁 gift-box bonuses (weekly Q&A, 1-on-1 onboarding call, community access), no pricing, CTA to
join free. Only the PAID About page uses this value-stack-with-prices format.
