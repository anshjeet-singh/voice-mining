# FRAMEWORKS INDEX: Read This First

This directory is the agency's distilled doctrine: 25 files mined word-by-word from the $100M playbooks (Offers, Leads, closing, promotions), Sell Like Crazy and the Suby system files, the Brunson books, SPIN Selling (Rackham), Advanced Language Patterns Mastery (McLaughlin), the ad-script and Haynes skill bundles, and a scavenge of Todd Brown (E5), Stefan Georgi (RMBC), Dan Henry, Jason Wojo, and Hormozi's own copy method.

You still read EVERY file. This index tells you which files carry the most weight for YOUR deliverable, and the universal quality gates that apply to everything.

## Universal quality gates (every deliverable, no exceptions)

1. Hormozi copy tests (dr-masters-scavenge.md section 5): 80% problem articulation to 20% solution. Observable Reality Rule: no sentence a court witness couldn't verify. Google Doc test: the words must sell on a plain white page.
2. Market intelligence IS the copy source. Verbatims from the research become the phrasing. No persuasion occurs in the vague.
3. Proof is real or placeholdered as [PROOF:]. Never fabricated. (hooks-and-proof.md, copy-quality-bar.md)
4. McLaughlin language QA (mclaughlin-language-patterns.md section 16): no negation installing the wrong picture, problem in past tense and result in present, every section ends on the wanted representation, embedded-command density low.
5. Third-grade reading level. Short syntax. NO em dashes anywhere.
6. Named unique mechanism: if the copy can't complete "the secret is in the...", the mechanism work is not done (dr-masters-scavenge.md sections 1 and 2).
