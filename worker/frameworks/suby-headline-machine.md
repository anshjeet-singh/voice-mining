# The Headline Machine (Sell Like Crazy, mined line by line)

This file is the LAW for every headline, subheadline, HVCO title, bullet, and
email subject. The generic "clear promise" bar produced clean-but-flat copy.
Flat copy is a defect. Every high-scoring headline does FOUR things at once:

1. **Hyper-specific dream outcome** (a number, a dollar range, a timeframe)
2. **A "without" clause** that kills the #1 objection in the same breath
3. **An "even if" clause** that pulls the disqualified reader back in
4. **A curiosity device** that makes NOT reading feel expensive: a named
   mechanism/loophole, a WARNING, a secret, an expose, a parenthetical tease

If a headline hits fewer than three of the four, it is not done.

## The process (mandatory)

Draft 10-15 candidates across the patterns below. Score each against the four
non-negotiables. Ship the top 5-7, ranked, chosen one first. Never ship the
first draft.

## The 8 proven patterns (with worked examples for a business-funding client)

1. **How To / Without / Even If** (the workhorse; ship this first):
   "How To Unlock $50,000 To $300,000 In Business Funding Without Paying A Dime
   In Fees Until The Money Hits Your Account (Even If You've Been Denied
   Everywhere Else)"
2. **Godfather offer** ("Give me X and I'll give you Y"):
   "Give Me One Free 15-Minute Call And I'll Tell You Exactly How Much Funding
   Your Business Can Pull, Then Walk You Into A Real Bank To Land It. And You
   Don't Pay My Fee Until The Money Is In Your Account."
3. **Little-known secret / named mechanism**:
   "The Little-Known 'Relationship Manager Method' Denied Business Owners Are
   Using To Walk Out Of Real Banks With $50K To $300K, While Everyone Else Gets
   Auto-Declined By An Online Form"
4. **WARNING pattern interrupt**:
   "WARNING: Do Not Submit Another Online Funding Application Until You Read
   This. Every Denial Is Quietly Making Your Next Approval More Expensive."
5. **Enemy expose**:
   "EXPOSED: Why Funding Mills Take $10,000 Up Front And Still Get You Denied,
   And The Banker-First Method That Gets The Exact Same File Approved"
6. **Call-out question + reveal**:
   "Sick Of Hearing 'No' From Banks? Discover What Your Business Can REALLY
   Pull When Your File Is Positioned Right, Sequenced Right, And Put In Front
   Of A Banker Who Wants To Say Yes"
7. **Contrarian reason-why** (the Big Domino as headline):
   "It's Not Your Credit. It's The Order You Apply In. Here's How Denied Owners
   Get Positioned For $50K To $300K By Fixing The Sequence, Not The Score."
8. **Desire / unlock**:
   "Unlock The $50K To $300K Sitting On The Other Side Of Your Next
   Application, Without Burning One More Hard Inquiry Trying To Guess Your Way
   There"

Adapt the SHAPES to each client with their numbers, their mechanism, their
enemy. Never reuse these sentences for other clients.

  Blow Your Mind!)"

## The headline essentials checklist (all four, every time)

1. Grabs them by the throat: jolts the sleepwalking scroller awake
2. NUMBERS: a count, a dollar figure, a timeframe (tangible + easy to follow)
3. Irresistible intrigue: power words + a twist/qualifier ("even if you don't
   know what to sell") + parenthetical tease where it fits
4. What's in it for THEM: the outcome is theirs, vivid and specific, never
   about the business

## Subheadline pattern (under the main headline)

Authority + reveal + stakes + zero-friction next step:
"[Authority figure] reveals [the named checklist/method] and the [dirty little
lies / hidden traps] that could cost you [specific dollar stakes] and stop you
from [dream outcome]. [Watch the video / Free report] reveals all."
Then leave nothing to question about the next step: "Click play, then pick a
time below. Takes 15 minutes."

## Fascination bullet formulas (offer stacks, benefit bullets, FAQ teasers, subjects)

- How to [X] without [Y]
- You need [X], right? WRONG! [Contrarian reveal]
- [N] ways to [Y]
- Where to find [Y]
- How to eliminate [X] forever
- What you should NEVER [do] when [situation] (fear of loss beats joy)
- Say goodbye to [frustration]
- The truth about [Y]
- Have you been doing [X] wrong? (And does it really matter?)
Every bullet teases; it never teaches. Curiosity is the incentive.

## The skepticism box ("This is why")

Cold readers scream "why are you telling me this?" Answer it on the page in
one honest box: why this information is free, what the business gets out of
it. Kills the objection that blocks every other word from landing.

## Enemy + Big Domino (carry through the whole funnel)

Name the enemy (the funding mills, the online form auto-decline, the outdated
counselling) and the ONE belief that, once accepted, makes buying inevitable
(the Big Domino: "it's not your credit, it's the sequence"). The headline
plants it, the VSL proves it, the FAQ defends it, the emails repeat it.
