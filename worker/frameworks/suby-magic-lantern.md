# The Magic Lantern Technique (Sell Like Crazy Phase 6, mined line by line)

The nurture machine for the 97% who opted in but did NOT book. Instead of
"just checking in" follow-ups, we lead them down a lit path of pure value
until booking becomes the obvious next step. This governs every nurture email
sequence, retargeting script, and mid-funnel video we write.

## The core mechanic

2-3 VIDEOS of pure value sent to non-bookers over the days after opt-in.
Each video:

- Delivers a REAL milestone toward their desired outcome (they are tangibly
  better off after watching, even if they never pay us)
- 80% value / 20% pitch, and the pitch is soft: "if you want help doing this
  faster, book a time here"
- Ends with the same single CTA as the funnel (book the named session)
- Is sequenced: video 1 solves the first obstacle between them and the dream
  outcome, video 2 the next, video 3 the next. Momentum builds belief.

Goodwill compounds: by giving away the best material free, the prospect
reasons "if the free stuff is this good, imagine the paid". That reasoning,
not pressure, books the call.

## Writing rules for Magic Lantern emails/scripts

- Each email sells the CLICK to the video, not the call (curiosity subject,
  cliff-hanger preheader, one command CTA)
- Reference the earlier video's win to build the streak ("you fixed X in the
  last video, this one handles Y")
- The pitch paragraph is always framed as speed/shortcut, never as need
  ("you now know the path; we walk it with you in 6 weeks")
- Milestones must be concrete: a number they calculate, an asset they draft,
  a decision they make. "Mindset shifts" are not milestones.

## Where this maps in our pipeline

- **Email sequences deliverable**: the non-booker branch IS a Magic Lantern
  arc (2-3 value emails/videos before any hard re-offer).
- **Video scripts deliverable**: mid-funnel scripts follow the 80/20 value
  structure with a milestone per video.
- **Skool free community**: pinned classroom content sequenced as milestones
  toward the paid community's dream outcome is the same technique in
  community form.
