# Lead Magnet Craft: the quality bar for free-community lead magnets

The three free-community lead magnets are the primary NURTURE ENGINE. Each is a
mini-course that lives inside the free Skool community and is forwarded to a
member stuck on a specific bottleneck. They must be genuinely valuable and deep,
never thin, never fluff. This file is the standard. Hold it every time.

## What each lead magnet contains (in this order)

1. **Course name** — max 50 characters (Skool's course-name limit). Named with a
   Suby/Hormozi framework: proof number or fear-of-loss or mechanism + speed/ease.
   Curiosity + specificity. Examples: "The Peptide Gold Rush Map", "The Never Get
   Pulled Playbook", "The $0 to First Commission Fast Start".
2. **Course description** — max 500 characters. Direct-response, benefit-forward,
   creates FOMO and willingness. One or two sentences on the outcome + what's inside.
3. **THREE content pages** — each a FULL in-depth lesson (see the page bar below).
4. **TWO worksheets** — structured fill-in exercises (see the worksheet bar below).
5. **A Loom script** — 400-650 words, spoken, film-ready (see the Loom bar below).

The three magnets are the SAME three named in the free community About page's ✅
"what you unlock for free" list. Tie each magnet to ONE of the ICP's top three
bottlenecks so each solves a different real problem.

## The PAGE bar (this is where most drafts fail)

Each page is a real course lesson. **AIM 700-1200 words. A thin ten-line page is a
FAIL.** Every page MUST contain, in a scannable form:

- A one-line **bold takeaway** at the very top (the whole point in one sentence).
- THOROUGH teaching: the what, the why it matters, and the exact HOW broken into
  clear steps. Explain it like they've never seen it, because they haven't.
- **TWO OR MORE worked EXAMPLES** with real numbers and named scenarios, laid out
  as markdown TABLES or boxed calculations the reader can copy. Show the math.
- A step-by-step **WALKTHROUGH** the reader can literally follow.
- A **common mistakes / what NOT to do** beat (3-4 bullets).
- A short **recap / "your move"** at the end that hands off to the worksheet.

Format for SCANNABILITY the whole way: sub-headings, **bold** on key terms, bullet
lists, numbered steps, tables, and `> ` blockquote CALLOUTS for the biggest ideas.
DEPTH AND SCANNABILITY TOGETHER. Never a wall of prose. Never ten thin lines.

## The WORKSHEET bar

A worksheet is a structured, print-ready fill-in exercise, NOT a bare list of
blanks and NOT a summary. Each worksheet:

- Opens with a one-line `*Purpose:*` statement.
- Is broken into **2-4 titled PARTS** (e.g. "Part 1: Learn the swap", "Part 2: Run
  your own", "Part 3: Self-check") so it flows like a real exercise.
- Contains inline GUIDANCE and at least one **worked EXAMPLE** (a filled row or line
  the member copies the pattern from).
- Has labeled fill-in fields with real blanks (e.g. `🎯 Target: $__________`) and,
  where it fits, a small fill-in TABLE with a header row and empty cells.
- Has `☐` tick boxes for a self-check.
- Ends with a bold `**Done when:**` outcome line.

It must feel worth downloading. It exports to a clean one-page PDF (spaced fields,
no walls of text).

## The LOOM bar

400-650 words, written the way the client SPEAKS (conversational, direct, operator
voice). Hook the bottleneck in the first line, tell them what they'll walk away
with, walk fast through the lessons, nudge them to do the worksheets, and close on
a soft CTA to book the free roadmap call or DM the keyword. Ends with a
`*CTA: ... [BOOKING LINK] ... [COMMUNITY NAME]*` line.

## Golden rules (non-negotiable)

- **NO em dashes anywhere.** Use "to" for ranges ("20 to 35%", "$80 to $150").
- **No fluff, no filler, no restated theory.** Every line earns its place.
- **Compliance-safe where the niche needs it** (peptides, health, finance):
  research-use-only framing, no human/medical claims, no income guarantees, add a
  "results not typical / education not advice" line where money or health is implied.
- Write in the CLIENT's real voice from the approved brand doc, grounded in the
  research verbatims and the Offers/ICP docs. Real proof only, else [PROOF: ...].
- Placeholders stay literal: `[BOOKING LINK]`, `[COMMUNITY NAME]`.

## Worked reference (this is the bar, match it)

### Page excerpt (note the takeaway, the table, the callout, the walkthrough)

> **The money is not in the first sale. It is in the reorder. Miss this and you will work ten times harder for the same money.**
>
> A buyer is not one sale, it is a monthly income stream: discovery, first order, reorder every month, then they stack a second and third product.
>
> **The formula:** `Monthly income = New buyers × Avg order × Your cut × (1 + reorder rate)`
>
> | Lever | Beginner | Month 3 |
> |---|---|---|
> | New buyers / month | 30 | 80 |
> | Avg order value | $90 | $120 |
> | Your cut | 25% | 25% |
> | Reorder rate | 20% | 40% |
> | **Monthly income** | **~$810** | **~$3,360** |
>
> 🔑 **Same person, 90 days, four times the income** by moving two levers, not by working harder.
>
> **Walkthrough:** before you promote, get four numbers: AOV, commission, reorder signal, payout terms. Plug them in, set a target, and you know exactly how many buyers a month you need.
>
> **Mistakes:** chasing one-time sales, vanity metrics, wrong niche match, ignoring the stack.
