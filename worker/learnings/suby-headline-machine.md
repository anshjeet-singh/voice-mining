# Learnings: suby-headline-machine

Accumulated craft lessons applied on every run.

- The four non-negotiables (dream outcome, without, even if, curiosity) can all be present and the headline can still be dead on arrival because of a weak LEAD VERB. Add a fifth scoring check: the opening verb must pull ("Unlock", "Land", "Walk Out With", "Steal Back"), never a flat state verb ("Get Positioned For", "Become", "Start Getting"). A client rejected a headline that passed all four elements purely on the limp "Get Positioned For $50,000..." opener.
- Add a "less is more" trim rule to the process: after scoring, cut every word that is not doing persuasive work and re-read aloud. Two of the eight patterns (WARNING interrupt, statement-plus-enemy) are strongest at their shortest; the workhorse How-To/Without/Even-If is the one place length is earned.
