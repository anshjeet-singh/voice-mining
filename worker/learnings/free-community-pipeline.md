- The skill is written as a strictly interactive, one-question-at-a-time advisor ("Do NOT dump everything at once," "Ask questions, get answers, then move forward"). Inside the orchestrator, all inputs (offer, ICP, proof, platform, founder, conversion mechanism) already exist in the approved foundation docs, so the interview is redundant and the interactive framing can stall a single-pass build. Add a "batch mode" note: when the required inputs are already available from upstream docs, map them to the Step 1 to 5 checklists and produce the Step 6 Community Pipeline Plan directly, without the back-and-forth.

- Inside the orchestrator, this skill's Step 6 Community Pipeline Plan is not a standalone deliverable: it composes as the final section of the skool-community-builder free-community document (name/tagline/About/VSL/config/pinned posts, then the pipeline plan). Note that when both skills run for one free community, their outputs merge into a single document rather than two, so the pipeline plan reads as the strategy layer beneath the launch assets.
