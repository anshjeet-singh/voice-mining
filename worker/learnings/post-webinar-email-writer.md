# Learnings: post-webinar-email-writer

Accumulated craft lessons applied on every run.

- The self-audit's "no discount mentioned unless pricing rule allows it" and dimensionalization rules transfer cleanly to a call-funnel post-call follow-up, but the skill's architecture is webinar-specific (attendee vs no-show, Phase 1/bridge/Phase 2 course). A one-line pointer that its no-show and non-buyer follow-up ANATOMY (one objection per email, soft-to-hard, proof dimensionalized, morning-value/evening-urgency) is reusable for call-funnel post-call recovery would save the next writer re-deriving it.

<!-- 2026-07-22 · job 900001 · client: Blake Matthews -->
- The skill's architecture is the Days 0-8 direct-sell-plus-5-day-course shape (Phase 1 / bridge / Phase 2). But a webinar funnel whose event drives to a BOOKED CALL (not a direct buy) needs a different, recurring shape the skill does not document: (a) a Post-Webinar ATTENDED track on an aggressive 3-to-8-hour cadence with explicit send-timing labels (+2h, +9h, +18h, +1d, +1d 8h...), 8 to 12 emails, each leading with a real case study then breaking exactly one final limiting belief, single CTA to book the call with the free community as the late soft option, and (b) a NO-SHOW REPLAY track of 10 to 14 emails across ~14 days where days 1-3 push a real time-boxed replay (e.g. 48h) and days 4-14 pivot to registering for the next live training. Codify both so the shape is not re-derived per client.
- For a webinar funnel that pitches a high-ticket offer priced only on the call, the performance guarantee MODEL belongs in the Post-Webinar ATTENDED track (those readers watched the pitch) but must be kept OUT of the NO-SHOW replay track (they have not seen the pitch yet) and out of pre-webinar reminders. Guarantee placement follows "has this reader seen the offer yet," not "is this reader warm." State this so the guarantee is not grafted onto every post-webinar send.
