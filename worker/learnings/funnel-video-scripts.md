# Learnings: funnel-video-scripts

Accumulated craft lessons applied on every run.

<!-- 2026-07-09 · job 60002 · client: Trent Kus -->
- OFFER_BREAKDOWN says "never drop the actual price" but, like the VSL skill, does not distinguish the price number from the pricing model. When the back-end/pay-on-results structure IS the offer's differentiator, the video must state that model to handle the cost objection, while still withholding the figure. Add the same number-vs-model clarification here.

<!-- 2026-07-09 · job 120004 · client: Trent Kus -->
- CALL_CONFIRMED mode and the confirmation-page hero-video concept (from confirmation-page-that-converts) describe the same asset from two skills, but neither cross-references the other, so a writer can produce a thin "thanks for booking" script that the confirmation-page skill explicitly says always fails. Add a one-line note in CALL_CONFIRMED that this IS the confirmation-page hero video, and for high-ticket skeptical traffic it should lean toward the Research-Assistance shape (send them to the breakouts, point at the real wins wall, help them do their due diligence) rather than a plain "watch your inbox" affirmation.

<!-- 2026-07-10 · job 180001 · client: Trent Kus -->
- CALL_CONFIRMED mode should explicitly name the "Research Assistance" hero-video shape from confirmation-page-that-converts for high-ticket, skeptical, burned-before traffic: send them to the breakouts, point at the real wins wall, and help them do their own due diligence ("go check my reviews, see it with your own eyes"). The current mode text describes the elements but does not flag that for skeptical high-ticket audiences the video's job is to arm the prospect's research, which is a distinct and stronger frame than "get informed before the call."
