# Learnings: content-ads-cold-traffic

Accumulated craft lessons applied on every run.

<!-- 2026-07-10 · job 180009 · client: Trent Kus -->
- The skill is written as an interactive step-by-step intake ("ask the user, confirm, move on") with no mode for producing a finished Forester campaign plan in one pass from already-approved foundation and funnel docs. A one-shot deliverable mode (consume the ICP, Offers, Brand, and funnel docs, decide the answers, ship the plan, no Q&A) would fit the agency's document-rules pipeline where option lists and interactive checklists are forbidden in a client-facing file.
- The skill has no compliance or platform-policy layer. For regulated niches (finance, credit, health) the targeting section is materially wrong without flagging Meta's Special Ad Category, and the whole plan can get disapproved regardless of budget or bin structure. Add a cross-reference: when the client is in a restricted category, run meta-ad-restrictions-prep alongside and note the Special Ad Category effect on cold targeting.

<!-- 2026-07-10 · job 210001 · client: Trent Kus -->
- The skill is written as an interactive step-by-step intake with mandatory Q&A and a "let me verify all constraints" open checklist. It has no one-shot mode for producing a finished client-facing campaign plan in a single pass from already-approved foundation and funnel docs. Add a deliverable mode that consumes the inputs, decides the answers itself, and ships the plan with the verification gate reproduced as a completed (all-boxes-checked) statement, not an open question set.
- The targeting section is materially wrong for any regulated niche (funding, credit, finance, health) because it never flags Meta's Special Ad Categories. For those verticals the CB1 "cold targeting" (lookalikes, detailed interest/demographic targeting) is restricted, and the whole plan can get disapproved regardless of budget or bin structure. Add a standing instruction: when the client is in a restricted category, run meta-ad-restrictions-prep alongside and note the Special Ad Category effect on cold targeting up front.
