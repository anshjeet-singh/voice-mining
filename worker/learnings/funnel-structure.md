- The spec's VSL-page anatomy (headline bar → subhead → CTA) is correct, but it should explicitly warn that the funnel's one Big Idea and the VSL page HEADLINE are often not the same sentence. When the Big Idea is a reframe/diagnosis, the headline must restate the same idea as a forward-looking promise while the video hook keeps the raw reframe. Without this, a writer copies the Big Idea straight into the headline slot and trips the copy-quality-bar anti-pattern.
