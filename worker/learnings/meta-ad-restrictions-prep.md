- This skill covers Meta's pixel/domain DATA restrictions only, but the orchestrator's ad stage instruction demands a "compliance gate" that strips quantified approval-adjacent claims, provider-performance ratios, and personal-attribute financial claims from ad COPY for regulated niches (funding, credit, finance, health). There is no ad-copy-compliance reference anywhere in the bundle to satisfy that gate. Add one: the personal-attribute rule (no "you" claim that implies knowing the viewer's credit or finances), the ban on guaranteed amounts/timelines/approvals, the ban on quantified approval-adjacent figures, and the safe reframes (capability statements, "run your own margins," real-and-screenshotted results with a "results vary" disclaimer). Without it, every writer reconstructs the same rules from client lessons.
