- Add a compliance guard for regulated niches (finance, credit, lending, health, legal). Clients in these spaces sometimes reveal methods that are non-compliant or illegal (e.g. this client openly described filing false FTC reports). The mechanism must be built ONLY on the legitimate, publishable version of what they do, and any exposure flagged for a human. Without this instruction the skill will happily name and sell a mechanism that can't legally ship.
